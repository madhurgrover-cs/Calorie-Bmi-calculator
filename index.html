Understanding Calories

Calories are units of energy your body uses for all activities. The three macronutrients provide different amounts of energy:

  • Carbohydrates: 4 calories per gram - Primary energy source
  • Proteins: 4 calories per gram - Building blocks for muscles
  • Fats: 9 calories per gram - Energy storage and hormone production

BMI Categories

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight:

  • Underweight: BMI below 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5-24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25-29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 and above

How They Relate

Calories and BMI work together for health management. Understanding your daily caloric needs based on your BMI category can help maintain a healthy weight and energy balance.
